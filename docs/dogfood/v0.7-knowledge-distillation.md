# v0.7.0 Knowledge Distillation Dogfood Report

## Scenario

A coupon-eligibility demand is advanced to closeout, then its closeout material is distilled into reviewable wiki candidates. A stable business candidate is promoted into the local wiki, an archive-only candidate is rejected, and the closeout evaluation confirms all wiki decisions are resolved.

## Flow

1. `devflow start --title "Coupon wiki"` created demand `coupon-wiki`.
2. The demand state was advanced to `closeout` (smoke bypasses the agent-driven workflow stages).
3. `devflow closeout --demand coupon-wiki --result "Coupon eligibility shipped" --knowledge "Active membership must be checked before coupon discount rules."` wrote `closeout.md` and `memory-candidates.md` with the stable knowledge bullet.
4. `devflow wiki distill --demand coupon-wiki` wrote `closeout-raw-log.md` and `wiki-candidates.md`. Two candidates were distilled: one stable business candidate (source: `memory-candidates.md`) and one archive-only candidate (source: `closeout.md`).
5. `devflow wiki list --demand coupon-wiki` listed both pending candidates.
6. `devflow wiki promote --demand coupon-wiki --candidate 1 --name coupon-membership-rule --by release` wrote `.devflow/wiki/coupon-membership-rule.md`, updated `.devflow/wiki/WIKI.md`, marked candidate 1 as promoted, and appended `wiki.candidate_promoted`.
7. `devflow wiki reject --demand coupon-wiki --candidate 2 --by release --reason "archive-only material"` marked candidate 2 as rejected and appended `wiki.candidate_rejected` without writing a wiki entry.
8. `devflow wiki search coupon` found the promoted entry by case-insensitive substring.
9. `devflow evaluate --demand coupon-wiki --stage closeout` reported `closeout.wiki_candidates` as pass and `closeout.wiki_decisions` as pass.

## Evidence

- `wiki-candidates.md` contains the promoted business candidate with `[promoted: .devflow/wiki/coupon-membership-rule.md]`.
- `.devflow/wiki/coupon-membership-rule.md` exists and contains the knowledge text.
- `.devflow/wiki/WIKI.md` links to `coupon-membership-rule.md`.
- `events.jsonl` contains `wiki.candidates_distilled`, `wiki.candidate_promoted`, and `wiki.candidate_rejected`.
- `evaluate --stage closeout` reports `closeout.wiki_decisions` as pass after all candidates are decided.

## Observations

- Distillation is deterministic: the same closeout material always produces the same candidates. No LLM or external call is involved.
- The archive-only candidate is created whenever closeout material exists and must be explicitly decided (promoted or rejected) for `closeout.wiki_decisions` to pass.
- Promotion slug validation rejected unsafe names (path separators, `..`, uppercase, dots) during unit testing.
- Wiki checks are warning-only and did not block closeout completion.

## Limits

- Substring search is not semantic; it cannot rank or relate entries.
- The wiki store is project-local under `.devflow/wiki`.
- Human review remains mandatory; distillation does not verify the truth of candidates.
