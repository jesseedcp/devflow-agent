# Devflow v0.7.0

## Summary

v0.7.0 adds a local long-term wiki / knowledge distillation loop: `closeout -> wiki candidate -> review -> promote`. Wiki candidates are distilled deterministically from closeout material, reviewed by a human, and promoted into a project-local searchable wiki store under `.devflow/wiki`. No LLM, embeddings, or external wiki write-back is involved.

## Changes

- Added `closeout-raw-log.md` and `wiki-candidates.md` as standard demand artifacts.
- Added a local wiki store (`.devflow/wiki/`) with safe slug validation and an index (`WIKI.md`).
- Added `devflow wiki distill` to deterministically extract stable business knowledge from memory-candidate bullets, process improvement signals from implementation-review recommendations and MR/return events, and an archive pointer from closeout material.
- Added `devflow wiki list`, `wiki promote`, `wiki reject`, and `wiki search` for human-gated candidate review.
- Recorded `wiki.candidates_distilled`, `wiki.candidate_promoted`, and `wiki.candidate_rejected` events.
- Added `closeout.wiki_candidates` and `closeout.wiki_decisions` closeout evaluation checks (warning-only, non-blocking).
- Surfaced wiki candidate counts (`pending`/`promoted`/`rejected`) in `status`, `console`, and `workbench --snapshot`.
- Closeout next actions now suggest `wiki distill` (candidates missing) or `wiki list`/`wiki promote` (candidates pending), and stay silent once all candidates are decided.
- Added a wiki knowledge distillation smoke to release-readiness.

## How It Works

- Distillation is deterministic text extraction, not semantic truth. It reads `closeout.md`, `memory-candidates.md`, `implementation-review.md`, and `events.jsonl`.
- Stable business candidates come from bullet lines under `## Stable Knowledge Candidates`, `## 稳定知识候选`, and `## Stable Business Knowledge` in `memory-candidates.md`.
- Process improvement candidates come from non-ready implementation-review recommendations and MR/review events containing `action_required`, `returned_to_requirements`, `returned_to_plan`, or `returned_to_implementation`.
- Archive-only material points to `closeout-raw-log.md` when any closeout material exists.
- Promotion writes `.devflow/wiki/<slug>.md` and updates `.devflow/wiki/WIKI.md`. Rejection records a reason without writing a wiki entry. Search is a case-insensitive substring scan over promoted entries (excluding the index).

## Known Limits

- Wiki distillation is deterministic text extraction, not semantic truth. Human review remains mandatory.
- Local wiki search is substring-based and intentionally not semantic or a dependency graph.
- `.devflow/wiki` is project-local; cross-project knowledge sharing remains out of scope.
- External wiki providers (Feishu, GitHub Wiki, Notion, Confluence) remain out of scope until a later adapter wave.
- Closeout wiki checks are warning-level in v0.7.0; promoting them to blockers should require dogfood evidence.
